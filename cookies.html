This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies we use on cg-pt.co.uk, why we use them, and how you can control them. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to remember your preferences, and sometimes to provide information to the website owner.

Cookies can be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use a limited number of cookies on this website. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies that follow you across the internet.

4. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Some cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off. Our cookie consent cookie (cgpt_cookie_consent) falls into this category — it is required to remember that you have already made a choice about cookies, so we do not repeatedly ask you.

Strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent under UK GDPR.

  • Accept — allows all cookies described in this policy to be set
  • Decline — we will still set the consent preference cookie so we do not ask again, but no optional cookies will be set

6. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

You can also control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Delete individual or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
