About Me

Your Coach,
Your Partner

Welcome to CG Performance Training — I'm a dedicated personal trainer based in East Kilbride, passionate about helping individuals unlock their full athletic and physical potential.

With a results-driven approach and genuine commitment to every client, I create personalised programmes that fit your lifestyle, goals, and current fitness level — whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned athlete looking to break through a plateau.

My coaching philosophy is simple: consistency, progressive overload, and the right support system. Every programme is built around you — your schedule, your goals, your body.
